About the Festival:

The Deposit Lumberjack Festival is a three-day fair held in Deposit, NY which includes foods, rides, a craft fair, and many other events -- in particular, the lumberjack competition.

There are many more events, including on the day of the race. Stick around after the race to check them out! Find out more here!
