Individual and team awards:

  • Trophies to the top runner in each age group
  • Top team of 3 participants receives a trophy
  • Certificates to all finishers in the 5k run and walk

- $20 race-day registration

- Questions? Email David Bergstrom at dfrg@pobox.com

View this Course on MapMyRun

Course description: The course starts at the southeast corner of Fireman's Field. You will be met with several moderate climbs shortly after the start and before the end of the first mile. The course will wind through the streets of Deposit towards the elementary and high schools. You'll run past the new running track which was partially funded by proceeds from previous Lumberjack 5k events. After a relatively flat second mile, prepare for some more climbs to round out the middle of the third mile, then descend back down to finish back at Fireman's field. See the map above for details.
